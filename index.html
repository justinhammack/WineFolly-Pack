Frequently Asked Questions

What states can you ship to?

We are only shipping domestically and can NOT ship to the following states: Alabama, Arkansas, Delaware, Kentucky, Massachusetts, Mississippi, New Jersey, New Mexico, Oklahoma, Pennsylvania, Rhode Island, South Dakota, Utah.

Please email us and let us know you'd like to arrange a local Seattle pickup. When everything is ready you will be contacted with a range of days that the wine will be available for pickup.

Does the price include taxes?

Taxes only apply to Washington residents and will be added during checkout.

Absolutely! The above packages are just for your convenience, feel free to source the wine from any retailer you'd like.

Your wine will be shipped 5-7 days before the release of the course, we expect this to be near the end of May.
